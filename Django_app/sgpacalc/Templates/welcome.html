Calculate your Semester Grade Point Average (SGPA) and Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) easily with this app.

This website is designed to help VTU (Visvesvaraya Technological University) students understand and compute their grades based on the VTU grading system. SGPA and CGPA are important indicators of academic performance in VTU's engineering and technical courses.

How SGPA is Calculated:

SGPA is calculated by dividing the sum of the product of credits and grade points obtained in each course by the total number of credits.

Formula:

SGPA = (Σ (Ci * Gi)) / Σ Ci

where:

  • Σ (Ci * Gi) = Sum of (Credits of the course * Grade points obtained in the course)
  • Σ Ci = Total number of credits of all courses
